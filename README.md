## What is an RS-232 Fiber Converter?

An RS-232 fiber converter, also called an RS-232 fiber modem, converts traditional RS-232 serial signals into optical signals for fiber optic transmission. These converters are ideal for extending serial communication over long distances, providing secure, low-latency, and fault-tolerant connections.
